Endlessity

2024 Video,TRT
6:52

Explore the world of synthetic materials and their applications. *Endlessity* continues Bailey’s exploration of digital identity through her avatar L00bi. Following the hyper-saturated chaos of Loobtopia (2023), a film about online performance and self-curation, *Endlessity* shifts into a quieter, limbo zone.

The film unfolds as an intimate, almost secretive dialogue between L00bi and a nonhuman presence, moving through glitchy landscapes, rave footage, and digital limbos. It reflects on longing, erasure, and what it means to remain human when our identities are constantly shaped by machines.

TRUST THE PROCESS

2024 Video,TRT
7:40

TRUST THE PROCESS (2025) is an AI-generated animation composed from Pinterest-sourced images and narrated by an AI-clone of the artist’s voice. Referencing a genre of YouTube content in which AI-generated Carl Jung personas narrate life advice, the piece moves through four sections—Flow, Alignment, Intuition, and Integration. It invites belief while unraveling its mechanics, offering a guided meditation that is both soothing and synthetic.

The work is part of REAL FEELINGS, a larger installation that explores how digital systems package relief as content—and how affirmation, even when automated, can still feel like recognition. The show asks what we reach for when we’re afraid, and what it means to find comfort in something designed to simulate it.
